plenty is a peer-to-peer app to manage food buyers clubs, so that eating amazing food from locally sourced producers becomes joyful, easy and cheap.

With it, you can add producers and their products' details, create orders from all the households that are members of the buyers club, and manage delivery and sorting tasks.

After doing user research, we have started software development of the prototype for plenty. Stay tuned for updates coming soon!
